SafePath Info & Tips

Disclaimer: This map reflects subjective, self-reported experiences submitted anonymously by community members. It is not verified by law enforcement and does not represent official crime statistics.

How SafePath Works

SafePath is a fully anonymous tool that empowers community members to document safety concerns in public spaces. No logins, accounts, or IP addresses are saved.

To submit, click anywhere on the map, select a category, and optionally add a brief note (max 100 characters). For privacy, submissions are automatically randomized by 50–100 meters, so exact coordinates are never stored.

Safety Recommendations

  • Trust your instincts. If an area feels unsafe, seek a well-populated, well-lit place immediately.
  • When traveling alone, share your real-time path with a trusted contact.
  • Stay alert: limit distractions like wearing headphones or staring at your phone in isolated spaces.
  • Identify "safe havens" like 24-hour convenience stores or public buildings on your route.
  • In case of immediate danger, dial emergency services (e.g. 911) rather than using this application.
